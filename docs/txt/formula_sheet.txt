# Formula sheet

> One page of the core equations only. No prose, no derivations.

---

### Substrate fluid (barotropic)

- **Continuity:**
  
  \[
  \partial_t n + \partial_j(n v_j) = 0
  \]

- **Momentum:**
  
  \[
  \partial_t(n v_i) + \partial_j\!\left(n v_i v_j + \delta_{ij}P(n)\right) = \mathcal{F}_i
  \]

- **Sound speed:**
  
  \[
  c_s^2 = \frac{dP}{dn} \le c^2
  \]

---

### Gravito-magnetic flows

- **Rotational (spin):**
  
  \[
  \omega_s(r) = \frac{2 G J}{c^2 r^3}
  \]

- **Translational (mass current, retarded):**
  
  \[
  \frac{1}{c^2}\frac{\partial^2 \mathbf{u}_g}{\partial t^2} - \nabla^2 \mathbf{u}_g = \kappa_J\,\mathbf{J}_{\mathrm{TT}},\quad \mathbf{J}=\rho\,\mathbf{v}
  \]

---

### Photon propagation (moving-medium Fermat)

- **Travel time functional:**
  
  \[
  T = \int n\,ds - \frac{1}{c}\int (n^2 - 1)\,(\mathbf{u}_g\cdot\hat{\mathbf{k}})\,ds
  \]

---

### Wave sector (tensor transverse–traceless)

- **TT wave equation:**
  
  \[
  \Box h_{ij}^{\mathrm{TT}} = \mathcal{S}_{ij}^{\mathrm{TT}},\qquad \Box \equiv \frac{1}{c^2}\partial_t^2 - \nabla^2
  \]

- **First-order variables:**
  
  \[
  p_{ij}=\partial_t h_{ij}^{\mathrm{TT}},\quad q_{kij}=\partial_k h_{ij}^{\mathrm{TT}}
  \]

---

### Symmetric-hyperbolic structure

- **State vector:**
  
  \[
  \mathbf{U}=(n,\ m_i,\ h_{ij}^{\mathrm{TT}},\ p_{ij},\ q_{kij}),\quad m_i\equiv n v_i
  \]

- **System form:**
  
  \[
  A^0(\mathbf{U})\,\partial_t \mathbf{U} + A^k(\mathbf{U})\,\partial_k \mathbf{U} = \mathbf{R}(\mathbf{U})
  \]

- **Symmetrizer (block-diagonal):**
  
  \[
  S_{\mathrm{fl}}=\mathrm{diag}\!\left(\frac{P'(n)}{n},\ \frac{1}{n}\mathbb{I}_3\right),\quad S_{\mathrm{TT}}=\mathrm{diag}\!\left(\mathbb{I},\ c^2\mathbb{I}\right)
  \]

- **Energy norm:**
  
  \[
  \mathcal{E}=\tfrac{1}{2}\left[\frac{P'(n)}{n}\,n^2+\frac{|m|^2}{n}+|p_{ij}|^2+c^2|q_{kij}|^2\right]
  \]

- **Energy identity and boundary flux:**
  
  \[
  \partial_t \mathcal{E} + \partial_k \mathcal{F}^k = \mathbf{U}^{\!\top}S\,\mathbf{R}(\mathbf{U}),\qquad \mathcal{F}^k=\tfrac{1}{2}\mathbf{U}^{\!\top} S A^k \mathbf{U}
  \]

- **Characteristic speeds:**
  
  \[
  \lambda_\pm=v_n\pm c_s,\ \lambda_0=v_n,\ \lambda_{\mathrm{TT}}=\pm c
  \]

---

### Cosmology-lite

- **Hubble law analogue:**
  
  \[
  H^2(a)=\left(\frac{\dot{a}}{a}\right)^2=\frac{8\pi G_{\mathrm{eff}}}{3}\rho_{\mathrm{tot}} - \frac{k}{a^2} + \frac{\Lambda_{\mathrm{eff}}}{3}
  \]

- **Distances:**
  
  \[
  D_C(z)=\int_0^z \frac{c\,dz'}{H(z')},\quad D_L=(1+z)D_C,\quad D_A=\frac{D_C}{1+z}
  \]

- **Growth (subhorizon):**
  
  \[
  \delta'' + \left(2 + \frac{H'}{H}\right)\delta' - \frac{3}{2}\,\Omega_m(a)\,\delta = 0,\quad (\,{}' \equiv d/d\ln a\,)
  \]
